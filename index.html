Run and Eat Healthy

Running is one of the most natural ways to exercise. I help beginners to take their first steps and to slowly climb without injury. It is important here to also look at the health of tendons, ligaments and above all cartilage depending on age and then to influence regeneration through nutrition. There is often hyperacidity, which is accompanied by inflammatory processes. A change in diet is recommended here before we increase the mileage. You can increase your muscular strength relatively quickly with structured training. Bradytrophic tissue such as tendons, ligaments and cartilage take significantly longer.With advanced users, we will work on effective increases in performance with optimized training plans. Here, too, nutrition plays an important role in regeneration. A few important principles: "no pain no gain" and "use it or lose it".When it comes to nutritional advice, I focus on a fully plant-based diet, but without completely dispensing with animal products. It's important to me that you understand what happens in your body when you eat too much meat or too many empty carbohydrates, for example. Oxidative stress and the body's various endogenous and exogenous ways of dealing with it are an important focus. The implementation in the kitchen naturally plays a major role.I advise part-time and on a voluntary basis, ie I work with few clients, but free of charge.Practice hours are in Erlangen / Nuremberg, but can also be carried out via Zoom or Skype when it comes to advice.
I'm 53 and come from competitive youth sports. I have a running trainer license C at the running campus in Euskirchen.
I am a multiple marathon runner. I trained as a nutritionist at the Academy of Naturopathy in Switzerland.
